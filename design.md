# Rekindle: Brand & Design Guidelines

This document outlines the core design language, color scheme, and aesthetic philosophy for the Rekindle app. Use these guidelines when building marketing materials, websites, social media posts, videos, or new app features to ensure a cohesive brand identity.

## 🧭 Core Philosophy & Vibe

**"Friendly, Soft, Inviting, and Low-Pressure"**

Rekindle is an app designed to solve a very human problem: we want to stay in touch, but life gets in the way, and reaching out can feel awkward or stressful. The design must counteract that anxiety.

*   **Warmth over Utility:** The app shouldn't feel like a sterile CRM or a high-pressure task list. It should feel like a warm nudge from a friend.
*   **Softness over Sharpness:** Hard lines, pure blacks, pure whites, and sharp corners introduce visual stress. We eliminate them in favor of pastels, off-whites, and hyper-rounded geometry.
*   **Playful, not Childish:** The UI is bouncy and colorful, but remains clean and modern enough for adults managing real relationships.

---

## 🎨 Color Palette

Our colors are purposefully muted and soft, avoiding harsh or hyper-saturated primaries.

### Brand Accents
These are the core identity colors used for logos, primary buttons, gradients, and main calls to action.
*   **Coral (Primary):** `#FA8585`
*   **Warm Orange:** `#FA9A72`

### The "Rekindle Gradient"
The signature visual of the brand, used on the app icon and massive CTA buttons.
*   **Start (Top Leading):** Coral (`#FA8585`)
*   **End (Bottom Trailing):** Warm Orange (`#FA9A72`)

### Secondary Accents
Used for avatars, playful UI elements, and specific status indicators.
*   **Amber:** `#FFC766` *(used for 'Postponed' states)*
*   **Soft Teal:** `#66D1CC`
*   **Sage Green:** `#8CBF9E` *(used for 'Done / Reached Out' success states)*
*   **Dusty Blue:** `#85ADE0`

### Semantic / Background Colors
We avoid `#000000` (pure black) and `#FFFFFF` (pure white) for vast backgrounds to reduce eye strain and keep the app feeling soft.

*   **App Background (Light Mode):** Creamy Off-White / Beige (`#FAF9F6`)
*   **App Background (Dark Mode):** Soft Charcoal (`#1C1C1F`)
*   **Card Fill (Light Mode):** Crisp White (`#FFFFFF`) — Used only on top of the creamy background to make elements lift off the screen.
*   **Card Fill (Dark Mode):** Lighter Charcoal (`#29292B`)

---

## 🔤 Typography

The typography reinforces the approachable, friendly aesthetic.

*   **Font Family:** Apple System Rounded (`SF Pro Rounded`).
*   **Weight:** We lean heavy. Marketing materials and headers should use **Bold** or **Heavy** variants to appear "bubbly" rather than thin, technical, or corporate. Body text should be **Medium** to ensure readability while maintaining softness.
*   **Usage in Marketing:** Never use rigid serifs or hyper-geometric harsh sans-serifs (like Futura or Helvetica). If SF Pro Rounded is unavailable for web/marketing, use alternatives like **Nunito**, **Quicksand**, or **Fredoka**.

---

## 🔲 Shapes & Geometry

Sharp corners do not exist in the Rekindle universe.

*   **General UI (Sheets, standard elements):** `24pt` border radius.
*   **Cards / Major Elements:** `32pt` border radius. We use "hyper-rounded" box designs to create pillowy, comfortable shapes.
*   **Buttons:** Always fully rounded / Capsule / Pill-shaped. Do not use slight rounding on buttons; the ends should be complete semi-circles.

---

## ☁️ Depth & Shadows

Rather than casting harsh directional black shadows, Rekindle elements "glow."

*   **Tinted Shadows:** Product cards and marketing assets should use shadows tinted with the primary **Coral** at low opacity (e.g., 10-15%).
*   **Button Glow:** Primary gradient buttons cast a pure Coral shadow directly underneath them to look alive and interactive.

---

## 📸 Guidelines for Marketing Materials

When creating Instagram stories, App Store screenshots, TikToks, or a landing page, adhere to these rules:

1.  **Backgrounds:** Use deep Charcoal (`#2C2C2E`) or Cream (`#FAF9F6`) to let the app screenshots and fiery gradient logo pop out.
2.  **Messaging:** Frame the problem simply and empathetically.
    *   *Instead of:* "Manage your contacts efficiently."
    *   *Say:* "When did you last text someone just to say hey?"
3.  **Visual Structure:** Keep it minimal. Let the hyper-rounded cards and soft UI speak for themselves. Avoid cluttering the design with abstract graphics.
4.  **Tone:** Optimistic, gentle, and encouraging. Never make the user feel guilty for losing touch with people; frame it as a universal struggle that Rekindle makes effortless.
